During the summer of 2025, I had the opportunity to participate in the **"Build Your Own Mobile Robot"** program at EPFL (École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne) in Switzerland. 

It was an incredible experience where I learned how to combine software and hardware. Over four days, we went from learning basic microcontroller programming to designing 3D chassis, filtering sensor data, and implementing artificial neural networks.
