During the summer of 2025, I had the opportunity to participate in the **"Build Your Own Mobile Robot"** program at EPFL (École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne) in Switzerland. 

It was an intense and incredible experience where I learned how to bridge the gap between software and hardware. Over four days, we went from learning basic microcontroller programming to designing 3D chassis, filtering sensor data, and implementing artificial neural networks.
